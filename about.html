About Europ.in

Europ.in is a service for the visual discover and exploration of Europeana content.

It starts with a simple search screen:

  • Enter search keywords and watch as images from Europeana appear before you in a continuous stream.
  • The item view shows detailed information about the image along with social sharing buttons and a link to this item on Europeana.
  • Share the image with friends using "Pin It" and "Facebook Like" buttons.

Europ.in was developed as a part of the #Hack4Europe Riga hackaton by:

  • Eriks
  • Maksim
  • Uldis
